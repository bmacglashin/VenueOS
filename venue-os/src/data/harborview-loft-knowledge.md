# Harborview Loft

Harborview Loft is a waterfront event venue designed for private dinners,
cocktail receptions, rehearsal dinners, and full-venue celebrations.

## Core facts

- Address: 240 Harbor Street, Brooklyn, NY 11201
- Neighborhood: Dumbo waterfront
- Phone: (718) 555-0140
- Email: events@harborviewloft.example
- Public hours: Wednesday through Sunday, 4:00 PM to 10:00 PM
- Monday and Tuesday are reserved for private events, load-in, and maintenance

## Event formats

- Rooftop buyouts are best for 40 to 120 guests
- Seated rehearsal dinners typically cap at 88 guests indoors
- Cocktail-style receptions can accommodate up to 140 guests when the rooftop
  and loft are both active
- The venue includes an elevator, ADA-accessible restrooms, and a weather backup
  lounge inside the loft

## Food and beverage policy

- Harborview Loft uses an in-house culinary and bar program
- Outside catering is not permitted
- Dessert from a licensed bakery is allowed with prior approval
- Beverage minimums and event pricing must always be confirmed by the events
  team before quoting a guest

## Guest logistics

- The nearest parking garage is Harbor Garage on Water Street
- Valet is available for events with at least 60 guests
- Load-in begins 90 minutes before the contracted start time
- Amplified music on the rooftop must end by 10:00 PM

## Frequently referenced answers

- Walk-in public service is limited to the published Wednesday-Sunday hours
- Rooftop events always require a weather contingency plan
- Outside catering requests should be escalated to the events team because the
  default answer is no
- Pricing, minimum spends, and custom floorplan requests need human review if a
  current approved event sheet is not already attached
